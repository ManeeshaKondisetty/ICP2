Must visit barbecue's in Kansas City!

Barbecue in Kansas City is a pride to most of the people here.It's not just an item on the menu, It’s a tradition, rich with history and culinary tradition that shapes the city’s dining landscape every day—and has since the early 1900s. Kansas has travellers coming from around the globe, drawn in by its smoky authentic aroma, just to get a little taste of KC’s acclaimed cuisine.

According to numerous food bloggers we have a ranking for the top 4 barbecues:

  1. Joe's Kansas City Barbecue
  2. Q39 South
  3. Arthur Bryant's
  4. Fiorella's Jack Stack
Must try items from the menu:
  • Burnt ends
  • Pork ribs
  • Brisket
  • Full Chicken
